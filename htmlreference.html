Is it Supported in HTML5?

This table will only cover more uncommon tags that I don't immediately know are supported because of the frequency with which they are used. I will also not be including tags that I immediately know are not supported because they were replaced roughly 50 million years ago, which is also circa when I memorized their relacements.

Tag Purpose Supported? Replacement
<abbr> Displaying the meaning of an abbreviation Yes N/A
<acronym> Displaying the meaning of an acronym No <abbr>
<address> Attributing content Yes N/A
<applet> Embedding an applet No <embed> or <object>
<strike> Crossing out text No <del> or <s>
