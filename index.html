Why We're All Here

My name is Melissa Calawa and I am a computer science major at Western Oregon University, set to graduate summer of 2018. During a computer science student's senior year at WOU, they participate in a year long Software Engineering course known as a Senior Project. This portfolio is where I will be posting my various homework assignments, as well as any helpful references about computer science.

A Little More About Me

I am a transfer student at Western and have done previous coursework at Portland State University, where I earned a BA in English. Between programming, I work as a tutor teaching various subjects related to my first degree, including literature and essay writing. A lot of people in technology look down on the liberal arts, but I believe that critical thinking skills and the ability to communicate in both speech and writing are vital skills that will serve you well no matter what field you enter.
